About us

Who are we?

Rotaract Club Skopje Centar is a dynamic group of dedicated individuals who believe strongly in the power of collective action to make a positive difference in the world. Although small in size, the club is composed of members from diverse backgrounds, each bringing a unique blend of skills, experiences, and perspectives from different educational and professional fields.


Despite these differences, we share a common purpose and a passion for service that unites us as a close-knit team. Together, we are committed to developing and implementing meaningful projects that address some of the most pressing issues facing our society today. These initiatives not only seek to meet immediate needs but also aim to lay the foundation for a brighter, more sustainable future for our community and beyond.


At Rotaract Club Skopje Centar, we believe that small, positive actions can have a far-reaching impact. Through our work, we hope to inspire others to join us in building a better, more inclusive world for everyone.
